Puvvi: My Little Friend Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Puvvi: My Little Friend handles user information.

Information We Collect

Puvvi does not collect personal information. The app does not require an account or sign-in and does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, or third-party tracking tools.

Information Stored on Your Device

To preserve your experience, the app stores information such as your pet's name, progress, needs, statistics, inventory, and settings locally on your device. This information is not sent to the developer or any third party.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send optional local reminders about your pet. These reminders are scheduled and processed on your device. The app does not send notification tokens or reminder data to a developer-operated server.

Internet Use and Third Parties

Puvvi does not transmit personal information to a server and does not share information with third parties. The app contains no advertising network, analytics SDK, or user-tracking service.

Device Permissions

Other than optional notification permission, the app does not request access to sensitive resources such as your camera, microphone, precise location, contacts, or photo library.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because no personal information is collected, children's personal information is not processed or shared.

Data Deletion

Locally stored app data can be removed by resetting the pet within the app or by deleting the app from the device.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app or its practices change. The current version will always be published on this page.
