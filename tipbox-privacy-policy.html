TipBox Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

TipBox is developed by Hakan Silek. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the TipBox iOS app.

Data Collection

TipBox does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The app does not require an account and does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or third-party data-collection SDKs.

Data Stored on Your Device

TipBox may save calculation history and preferences such as your default tip percentage, split count, appearance, and haptic setting. This data is stored locally on your device using Apple-provided storage and is not transmitted to Hakan Silek or any third party. You can remove this data by clearing the app's history or deleting the app.

Network Access and Third Parties

TipBox does not require an internet connection for its core features and does not send your bill amounts, tip calculations, preferences, or usage activity to external servers. The app does not contain third-party analytics or advertising services.

Children's Privacy

TipBox does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Security

Because TipBox keeps its app data on your device, that data is protected by the security features of your device and operating system. You are responsible for maintaining appropriate security for your device.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when TipBox's features or data practices change. The latest version will be published on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact silekhakan@gmail.com.
