Sarman's Adventures Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Sarman's Adventures is an iOS game developed by Hakan Silek. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the game.

Data Collection

Sarman's Adventures does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. The game does not require an account and does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or third-party data-collection SDKs.

Data Stored on Your Device

The game stores gameplay data—such as level progress, checkpoints, scores, high scores, remaining lives, and unlocked levels—only on your device using Apple-provided local storage. This information is not transmitted to Hakan Silek or any third party. You can remove the locally stored data by deleting the app.

Network Access and Third Parties

The game's core features do not require an internet connection. Your gameplay activity, scores, and progress are not sent to external servers. The app does not contain third-party analytics or advertising services.

Children's Privacy

Sarman's Adventures does not knowingly collect personal information from children or other users. The game does not provide an account, chat, or form through which personal information can be submitted.

Security

Because gameplay data remains on your device, it is protected by the security features of your device and operating system. You are responsible for maintaining appropriate security for your device.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the game's features or data practices change. The latest version will be published on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact silekhakan@gmail.com.
